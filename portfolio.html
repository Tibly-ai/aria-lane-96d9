The Road So Far

Highlights from past shows

A few standout nights from ten years and 350+ performances — the ones that still give me chills.

August 2025

Main Stage — Riverfront Summer Fest, Portland

Second year invited back to the festival main stage, this time closing the Saturday lineup with my full six-piece band before a crowd of 3,000. The soul medley from this set is the most-shared video I've ever posted.

December 2024

Meridian Group Annual Gala — 8th Year Running

My eighth consecutive year as the featured entertainment for Meridian's black-tie awards night — a standards-heavy set for 400 guests at the Portland Art Museum's ballroom.

June 2024

100th Wedding — Cascade Ridge Estate

A milestone I never expected to count: my one-hundredth wedding performance. The couple asked for an obscure 1960s B-side for their first dance. We learned it, arranged it for trio, and there wasn't a dry eye in the tent.

October 2023

Monthly Residency — The Velvet Note Lounge

Launched an ongoing last-Friday residency after a one-off booking sold the room out. Two years on, it's still the night I test new arrangements and debut originals in front of Portland's warmest crowd.

May 2022

National Anthem — Providence Park

Sang the anthem a cappella for 25,000 soccer fans. Ninety seconds, no band, no safety net — still the single most exhilarating minute and a half of my career.

2015

Where It Started — Tuesday Nights at Marlow's

My first paid gig: a $50 Tuesday slot in a Portland jazz room, singing standards to a dozen regulars. I kept that first crumpled bill. Everything on this page grew from that stage.
