About Aria

Ten years of stages, one unwavering love for the song

I started singing in smoky jazz rooms in Portland at nineteen, and I've spent the decade since performing everywhere from candlelit wedding chapels to festival main stages. My range moves comfortably from Etta James to Adele, from Sinatra standards to today's charts.

What sets my performances apart isn't just the voice — it's the preparation. Before every booking, we talk. I learn your must-play songs, read your crowd, and arrive with a set designed for your night, not a generic playlist.
